Prism

Prism is a total quality of care management system for assisted living homes. It is a physical item installed in resident rooms that collects quantitative data using rudimentary sensors and integrates with existing quality management software programs to simultaneously recognize safety threats and patterns in nursing care. These patterns are continuously analyzed and relayed to key care stakeholders to improve the quality of care provided by the home and to relieve the stress of those most invested in a patient’s care. Middle River Aircraft Systems (MRAS)

Process Consultant • Jan. 2018 - May 2018

Middle River Aircraft Systems (MRAS) is a subsidiary of GE Aviation and one of the world’s leading suppliers of jet engine nacelles (engine housings) and thrust reversers (jet brakes) for both General Electric and Pratt & Whitney engines. They were looking to ramp up production in the Airbus A320 program and in order to accomplish this task, production bottlenecks must be minimized. My team and I were tasked to support MRAS in meeting the demands of an increased production schedule by optimizing part flow from the stockroom to assembly area in the A320 program.
